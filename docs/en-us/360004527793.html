I want to sell my eBlocker.

If you want to resell the eBlocker, we recommend the following steps before shipping:

1. Restore the eBlocker's factory settings.

Please follow these steps:

  • Create an empty file named "eblocker-factory-reset.txt" on the USB stick.
  • Disconnect the eBlocker from the power supply.
  • Connect the USB stick to the lower USB port of the eBlocker.
  • Connect the eBlocker to the power supply.
  • The factory settings will now be restored. After approx. 5 minutes the eBlocker should turn off again (no LED activity).
  • Remove the USB stick.

2. Write us an email to support@eblocker.com:

We need the following information from you to reassign the license key in our license database:

  • The first three blocks of the license key.
  • The serial number of your eBlocker (located at the bottom of the eBlocker).
  • Your email address with which you activated the eBlocker license.
  • The email address of the new owner, so that they can activate the eBlocker with the license key.

We will contact you as soon as we have changed the license.
