eBlocker Help > FAQs > Anon function (Tor-network)

Delays and video streams stop loading.

Verify if you have enabled the eBlocker's Tor-network (Anon function). If you have activated the Tor-network, please deactivate it.

The Tor-network does not guarantee fixed bandwidths of its Exit Nodes. Therefore, it may take a long time before a website is fully loaded, or video streams may stop loading.
