Admin locked out from Parental Control.

You have added all devices to a Parental Control profile and thus have no longer access to the eBlocker settings. 

To open the eBlocker settings you can either enter the eBlockers IP address (IP address:3000 - Example: http://192.168.1.26:3000), or the eBlocker emergency IP Address(http://169.254.94.109:3000) in your browser.
